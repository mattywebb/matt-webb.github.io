Telstra Sportsfan UI/UX

Telstra Media approached us looking for UI/UX experts and a design partner to assist them in the re-design and re-development of their Bigpond sport channel now known as Sportsfan. Over a 12 month period we undertook a variety of very in depth tasks such as; from scoping, Information Architecture, which included large scale wireframes and sitemaps, through to the finished design for the Desktop version. The site is responsive down to Tablet along with a dedicated Smartphone version.
